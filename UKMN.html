Website forUK Mask Network

Information site

Background

UK Mask Network is an organisation that advocates for the safety and scientific education of the UK, aiming to raise awareness of the importance and effectiveness of mask-wearing in public spaces. During the lockdown, I picked up this project because it was something I also believed in. This was during a time where there were still a lot of scepticism on wearing a mask as it was still the early stages of the Coronavirus pandemic. Through this role, I have learned more about web design best practices as well as gained more knowledge about mask usage.

Homepage

What was my role?

As a website designer, I worked with another designer and illustrator to create an easy to navigate website for UK Mask Network.

Using WIX was ideal for this organisation, as it allows for my client to be able to take it over once I've completed my project. The goal

A fully functioning website that allows people to read more about the organisation, tips on how to wear a mask, mask science, latest government news and to be able to contact them with ease. A website that is easy to navigate for all ages and walks of life.
