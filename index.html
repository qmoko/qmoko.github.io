Who I am

Designer & developer based in Nottingham

Just your average girl with a keen interest in web design and development! I've always enjoyed the process of website design since I had my very first blog as a teen and decided to dabble in some programming as of recent!

Fun fact: Despite studying IT in University, I only just started truly enjoying code after I graduated. This eureka moment came to me during my first freelance job. Turns out, I actually quite enjoyed the times I had to mess about with the CSS and PHP of the web pages! Everytime I tapped 'inspect' on Chrome, I found myself fascinated with what goes on in the background of a website. I fell into the YouTube rabbit hole, where I watched countless tutorials - eager to learn even more.

Ever since then, I've been passionate about creating websites, wanting to develop my front-end skills even further. Have a wander here and you'll find a compilation of projects I have gotten involved with!
