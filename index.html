My Work

Who is Qonita?

Designer & developer based in Nottingham

An excitable being who enjoys working with digital graphics as well as developing websites! I come from an IT background, so I love a bit of a techy challenge but I also have a creative side that is bursting to find an outlet!

What drove me to website development?

During my first freelance job, I found that tweaking the CSS and PHP files had ignited something in me. Whenever there were issues, I inspected a page and found myself fascinated with what goes on in the background of a website. Not only did this ignite the problem solver within, but playing with CSS to get the design to look and feel exactly how the client wants made me very happy!

I had learnt the fundamentals of programming during my studies but only really got to develop (pun intended) these skills further through my projects!

And so, I fell into the YouTube rabbit hole - going through tutorial after tutorial. I decided to practice further using freecodecamp - eager to learn even more!

Ever since then, I've been passionate about creating websites, wanting to develop my front-end skills even further. Have a wander here and you'll find a compilation of projects I have gotten involved with!
