Properties E-Commerce Website

Background

Lace Market Apartments specialise in serviced apartments for professionals travelling for business or leisure. Their business model is to provide guests with a comfortable, trustworthy and reliable stay in the heart of Nottingham. They were in need of an updated website and hired me to take their website to a new level. Their main requirements is having all their properties clearly listed on their website and to have a booking system that allows guests to book directly from their website.

Homepage

What was my role?

As their Website Developer, I took charge in revamping their website. This included all design and development stages.

This project required me to work closely with the social media manager and my managers so that I inputted the correct and most up to date information for the website.

Lace Market Apartment needed a new website to keep up to date on their properties and latest news about what is going on nearby.
